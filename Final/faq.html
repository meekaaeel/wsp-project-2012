Q. Why is there such a big range in the price of Mattresses?

This is a great question. Price is primarily a function of the quality of the item, materials being used and type of construciton. All beds are made with different types of foams for example. Some manufactureres use 1-2 layers; others use 6 or 7. This is why you have different feel and comfort levels. Foam density and quality is a key factor. Higher priced beds use better quality foams. National brands tend to be higher priced as they invest heavily in advertising and other forms of marketing there by passing this cost on to you in higher prices. That is why you need to try it out. The number one rule in purchasing a mattress is "how it feels to you". Buy what you like and is comfortable; not what an advertisement says.

Q. Why is there such a big range in the price of Furniture?

Much like mattresses above the price of furniture is based on quality of materials, workmanship, finsihings and overall quality and style of the pieces. Most of our Bedroom Sets and Furniture are made with solid wood and/or hardwoods. They will likely have Dovetail joints and reinforced runners for many years of performance. Lower priced Bedroom sets are made from synthetic materials like particle boad, compressed paper and synthetic veneers. These will perform well but will lack the uniqueness of natural woods both in finishes and performance.
